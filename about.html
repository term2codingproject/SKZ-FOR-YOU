‘Stray Kids’ (also commonly abbreviated as SKZ) is a South Korean boy band under JYP Entertainment and Republic Records. The group currently performs as an 8 member group including members Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N. The group was formed through the survival show ‘Stray Kids; in 2017, and officially debuted on March 25, 2018.
